# Get Started

The infrastructure exists. The ATS is registered with the SEC. The transfer agent is registered with the SEC. The broker dealer is a FINRA/SIPC member. Units are trading. Settlement is live.

## For allocators

If you allocate to private equity or private debt and you are tired of seven year lockups, stale quarterly marks, and GP discretion over your exit timing, this is built for you.

Vex units settle atomically. Price discovery is continuous. You can exit on the order book, not on a GP's timeline. Every position follows the same legal structure, the same unit standard, and the same data model, which means your operational overhead does not scale with your number of positions.

Qualified Purchasers ($5M+ in investments for individuals, $25M+ for institutions) can participate immediately.

## For companies

If you want to offer employees and early investors real liquidity without going public, filing an S-1, or submitting to quarterly earnings pressure, this is built for you.

Vex acquires equity through primary and secondary channels and creates a liquid market for units representing ownership in that position. Your cap table stays clean. Your governance stays intact. Your employees get liquidity.
